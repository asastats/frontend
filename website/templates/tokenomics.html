Understanding the Utility, Supply, and Governance Model Behind ASASTATS

Token Overview

• Token Name: ASASTATS
• Asset ID: 393537671
• Total Supply: 1,000,000,000 (fixed, non-mintable)
• Decimals: 6
• Immutable: No freeze or clawback functions

ASASTATS is the utility token that powers the ASA Stats platform. It enables access to premium services, participation in governance, and serves as a mechanism to reward contributors, developers, and liquidity providers.


Token Utility

ASASTATS is used for:

• Subscription payments to unlock premium features and tools
• DAO governance access (staking-based, now closed)
• Developer access to advanced API endpoints and publishing widgets
• Reward distribution for bug bounties, feature suggestions, and community initiatives
• Liquidity incentives for ASASTATS trading pairs


Supply Distribution

The token was distributed with transparency and long-term alignment in mind:


Allocation Amount Purpose
Development Pool 20% (200M) Fund ongoing feature rollouts and infrastructure scaling
Administration/Legal 12% (120M) Pre-DAO entity setup and operational costs
Team Allocation 12% (120M) Fully vested in 24 tranches, now complete
Community Rewards 15% (150M) Reward GitHub contributions, bug reports, and DAO discussion support
Airdrops 8% (80M) Growth campaigns, early user incentives
Giveaways 2% (20M) Developer grants, contests, awareness efforts
DAO Treasury (residual) TBD Unspent pools flow into the DAO treasury post-launch
